# ADRAGON Personal Technology Brand

register: brand

## Product Purpose

ADRAGON is a bilingual personal technology portfolio for a solo builder working on AI solo-company operating systems, agent audit protocols, evidence-led tools, and learning systems. The homepage should make the visitor feel that the work is serious, traceable, and unusually hands-on, not like a generic freelancer profile.

## Users

- Technical collaborators, hiring partners, and founders who need to understand the builder's taste, judgment, and project range quickly.
- Chinese and English readers who may land on either the static HTML page or the Next.js bilingual routes.
- Visitors who want proof of real systems before they decide to contact the builder.

## Brand Voice

Warm, mechanical, and careful. The site should feel like a maintained personal lab: precise enough for engineering readers, but not cold or corporate. Copy should be direct, compact, and free of hype.

## Strategic Principles

- Lead with systems, not services. The projects are the proof.
- Show the Solo AI Company OS image as the main artifact because it reveals how the builder thinks.
- Treat private or unpublished projects with restraint. Use case-preview language and never expose local paths or private repository URLs.
- Keep bilingual content first-class. Chinese should not feel like a translation afterthought.
- Prefer evidence, memory, audit, solo-company leverage, and learning-loop language over generic AI buzzwords.

## Anti-References

- Generic Upwork profile pages.
- SaaS hero templates with floating cards and oversized metrics.
- Neon cyberpunk AI pages that use darkness as a costume.
- Portfolio grids where every card has the same icon, title, and paragraph rhythm.

## Success Criteria

- The first viewport communicates a distinctive personal AI systems brand.
- Five projects are visible and scannable.
- Public links go to GitHub, private work stays as case preview.
- The static `index.html` and the Next.js source stay aligned.
- The page remains readable on mobile without overflow or cramped Chinese text.
